Every month we have a little competition of sorts. It's called the emissary ledger and while it does exist for every trading company the Reapers Bones is oviously the best and only company you will use making the others useless. Anyway, to participate in this monthly activity all you need to do is to fly our fantastic emissary flag and as a little bonus we have a special suprise for the great person who decides to do so. Since we are all about being real pirates we decided to make you visible to all other ships thus increasing the suspense AND it makes people more likely to fight you which is always fun. There's oviously a price for the different rank the you can get to unless you just get the follower rank also know as the first rank where yo get nothing. As a minor reward for supporting the reapers and getting to the Servant rank you will get some titles but still nothing special. If you manage to get to the Keeper rank we will not only reward you with simple titles but also with ship parts so you can honor the reapers even more than you already did.
